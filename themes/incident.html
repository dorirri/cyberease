FAQ

Incident management and threat response is a set of procedures and tools for detecting, analyzing, and remediating cyber threats and security incidents. The goal is to minimize damage, quickly restore systems and prevent future incidents from recurring.

We provide 24/7 incident response, allowing us to respond quickly to threats and incidents. Our team immediately begins analyzing and remediating the problem upon receiving a security breach.

We use advanced security monitoring systems such as SIEM (Security Information and Event Management), network traffic analysis tools and automated anomaly detection systems. This allows us to detect threats in real time and prevent incidents before they escalate.

During an incident, our team actively engages with your IT team to ensure complete transparency. We provide information on the current threat status, remediation steps, and additional actions to be taken.
