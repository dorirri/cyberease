FAQ

Cybersecurity training is a program designed to increase employee awareness of cyber threats and develop the skills needed to protect information. Training includes learning how to prevent attacks, recognize phishing, handle data securely, and adhere to corporate security policies.

Employees are often targets of cyberattacks such as phishing, social engineering or trust attacks. Trained employees are better able to protect corporate data, recognize threats and act appropriately.

It is recommended to conduct training at least once a year. It is also a good idea to conduct additional training when new threats emerge or when corporate security policies change, so that employees are always aware of current threats and protection measures.

We offer adaptable training programs that can be tailored to your company and industry. This includes a focus on the specific types of threats your business may face, as well as customized scenarios and cases based on real incidents.
