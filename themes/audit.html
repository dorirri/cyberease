FAQ

An information security audit is a comprehensive assessment of your organization's IT infrastructure to identify security vulnerabilities and risks. The audit examines systems, policies and processes to ensure they comply with information security standards.

Our experts conduct audits based on international information security standards such as ISO/IEC 27001, NIST (U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology), and other regulations and standards relevant to your region and industry.

The duration of the audit depends on the complexity and scope of your IT infrastructure. On average, an audit can take from 1 to 3 weeks, depending on the scope of work and the required level of analysis depth.

To conduct an audit, we will ask for access to your company's key systems, networks and security policies. This may include servers, network devices, access control documentation, and current security processes.
