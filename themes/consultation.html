Privacy consulting is a service that helps organizations comply with international privacy standards such as GDPR and CCPA. We analyze your company's current policies and procedures, identify vulnerabilities and offer recommendations to protect your customers' personal data.

Our experts help you comply with global data protection standards such as GDPR (European Union General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Protection Act), as well as other local regulations where applicable.

The consultation includes analyzing current data protection systems, developing recommendations for improving privacy policies, planning data security measures, and implementing technologies that minimize the risks of leakage and privacy breaches.

The length of the consultation depends on the complexity and size of your business. On average, a consultation can take from a few days to two weeks, depending on the amount of data and systems to be analyzed.
